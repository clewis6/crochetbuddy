Adorable Crochet Bunny Pattern - Free Tutorial

By Sarah Mitchell 15 min read Beginner Friendly

Create this absolutely adorable crochet bunny with our comprehensive step-by-step tutorial! This amigurumi pattern is perfect for beginners and makes a wonderful gift for children or a charming addition to your home decor.

Materials You'll Need

üß∂ Yarn

  • Worsted weight yarn in white (100g)
  • Small amount of pink for nose
  • Black for eyes and details

ü™° Supplies

  • 4.0mm crochet hook
  • Yarn needle
  • Stitch markers
  • Scissors

üß∏ Stuffing

  • Polyester fiberfill
  • Safety eyes (12mm)
  • Optional: wire for ears

Pattern Details

Finished Size: Approximately 8 inches tall

Skill Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Time to Complete: 4-6 hours

Stitches Used: Single crochet (sc), increase (inc), decrease (dec), magic ring

Common Abbreviations

sc single crochet
inc increase (2 sc in one stitch)
dec decrease (sc two stitches together)
st(s) stitch(es)
rnd round

Head (Start with White Yarn)

  1. Rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring [6]
  2. Rnd 2: inc in each st around [12]
  3. Rnd 3: (sc, inc) x 6 [18]
  4. Rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) x 6 [24]
  5. Rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) x 6 [30]
  6. Rnd 6: (4 sc, inc) x 6 [36]
  7. Rnd 7-12: sc in each st around [36] (6 rounds)
  8. Rnd 13: (4 sc, dec) x 6 [30]
  9. Rnd 14: (3 sc, dec) x 6 [24]
  10. Insert safety eyes between rounds 9 and 10, about 7 stitches apart
  11. Rnd 15: (2 sc, dec) x 6 [18]
  12. Stuff the head firmly
  13. Rnd 16: (sc, dec) x 6 [12]
  14. Rnd 17: dec around [6]
  15. Fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing

Body

  1. Rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring [6]
  2. Rnd 2: inc in each st around [12]
  3. Rnd 3: (sc, inc) x 6 [18]
  4. Rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) x 6 [24]
  5. Rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) x 6 [30]
  6. Rnd 6-10: sc in each st around [30] (5 rounds)
  7. Rnd 11: (3 sc, dec) x 6 [24]
  8. Rnd 12-13: sc in each st around [24] (2 rounds)
  9. Rnd 14: (2 sc, dec) x 6 [18]
  10. Stuff the body firmly
  11. Rnd 15: sc in each st around [18]
  12. Fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing to head

Ears (Make 2)

  1. Rnd 1: 4 sc in magic ring [4]
  2. Rnd 2: inc in each st around [8]
  3. Rnd 3: (sc, inc) x 4 [12]
  4. Rnd 4-8: sc in each st around [12] (5 rounds)
  5. Rnd 9: (2 sc, dec) x 3 [9]
  6. Rnd 10-11: sc in each st around [9] (2 rounds)
  7. Do not stuff the ears
  8. Flatten and sew to the top of the head

Pro Tips for Success

üí°

Tension Matters

Keep your stitches tight to prevent stuffing from showing through. Your hook size may vary based on your tension.

üìç

Use Stitch Markers

Mark the beginning of each round to keep track of your progress. This is essential for amigurumi!

üßµ

Weave Ends Securely

Make sure to weave in all ends securely, especially for toys that will be handled frequently.

‚ú®

Customize Colors

Feel free to make your bunny in any color! Gray, brown, or even rainbow colors work beautifully.

Assembly Instructions

1

Attach the Head

Sew the head securely to the body using the long tail. Make sure it's centered and firmly attached.

2

Position the Ears

Flatten the ears and sew them to the top of the head, angling them slightly outward for a cute look.

3

Embroider the Face

Use pink yarn to create a small nose and black yarn for whiskers. Add any other facial details you like!

4

Final Touches

Add any accessories like a bow tie, ribbon, or even a tiny carrot. Get creative!
